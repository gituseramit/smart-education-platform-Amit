Project Overview

The Smart Education Platform is an integrated MERN-stack ecosystem designed to support modern students through AI-driven tutoring, community engagement, and holistic mental health resources.

Core Objectives

  • Provide 24/7 personalized tutoring via Generative AI.
  • Connect students with verified industry mentors.
  • Foster peer-to-peer learning through community forums.
  • Support mental well-being via specialized counseling modules.
  • Streamline career growth with an integrated internship board.

Technology Stack

Frontend

React 19, Vite, TailwindCSS

Backend

Node.js, Express.js

Database

MongoDB Atlas

AI

Google Gemini / OpenAI

System Modules

1. AI Tutoring System

Integrates the latest Google Gemini model to provide real-time academic assistance, answering complex queries and generating study summaries.

2. Mentorship Platform

Allows students to discover mentors by industry, request guidance sessions, and track their professional growth.

3. Community & Collaboration

A full-featured forum where users can post questions, share resources, and interact with peers globally.

4. Mental Health & Wellness

A safe space for students to access counseling resources, perform mood tracking, and participate in secure video/audio calls with professionals.

5. Career Hub

Aggregates internship listings, company profiles, and application tracking in a single centralized location.

Architecture & Security

MERN Architecture

The platform follows a standard layered architecture with a clear separation between the client-side React application and the server-side Express API, ensuring scalability and maintainability.

Security Features

  • JWT for stateless authentication.
  • Secure cookie storage for sensitive tokens.
  • Password hashing using Bcrypt.js.
  • Role-based access control (RBAC).
